# Decoder

$$\begin{gathered}
\mathcal{D}=\{x^i,y^i\}_{i=1}^N \\
x^i=\{x^i_1,\cdots,x^i_m\}\text{ and }y^i=\{y_0^i,y_1^i,\cdots,y_n^i\}, \\
\text{where }y_0=\text{<BOS>}\text{ and }y_n=\text{<EOS>}.
\end{gathered}$$
